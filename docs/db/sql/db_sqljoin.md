
## ** SQL Joins & Relationships**  
- `INNER JOIN` – Matching records  
- `LEFT JOIN` / `RIGHT JOIN` – Including unmatched records  
- `FULL OUTER JOIN` – Combining everything  
- `SELF JOIN` – Joining a table to itself  
- `CROSS JOIN` – Cartesian product
